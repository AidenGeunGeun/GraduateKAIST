# Wave 2 Reviewed Interpretation Notes

## AE

- `2019-2021`: reviewed from the `2016학년도 이후` AE section with 42 major credits, 21 required-major credits, seven explicit AE required slots, and 18 심화 credits. The 2019 bulletin tree does not expose AE as a department node, but the same official requirement content is present in captured 2020-2025 documents under the `2016학년도 이후 입학 학사과정용` section, which governs 2019 admissions.
- `2022`: keeps the same AE core but adds the 2022-onward basic-selection note, `CoE491` elective recognition, and the first AE Co-op replacement rule.
- `2023-2024`: moves to the `2023학년도 이후` AE section with 138 total credits and the 21-credit AE Co-op pattern; 2024 adds a research-credit clarification.
- `2025`: same rule family as 2023+ but with the new dotted code family (`AE21000`, `AE22000`, ...).
- Manual review remains for 500-level 심화전공 mutual-recognition courses and the 2025 semester-specific research-credit edge case.

Example: `AE210` or `AE21000` is explicitly replaceable by `ME211` or `ME20011` in the matching code family slice.

## ME

- `2019-2020`: reviewed from the base post-2016 ME section with four fixed required slots and a nine-course basis pool. The 2019 bulletin tree does not expose ME as a department node, but the same official requirement content is present in captured 2020-2025 documents under the `2016학년도 이후 입학 학사과정용` section, which governs 2019 admissions.
- `2021-2022`: adds the explicit external-credit policy split for 2021+ admissions: at most 3 CoE elective credits, no other external-major credits.
- `2023-2024`: keeps the 2021+ ME structure but upgrades ME Co-op to the 21-credit family with a pre-2023 grandfather clause.
- `2025`: same rule family as 2023-2024 but with dotted codes (`ME.20000`, `ME.20005`, ...).
- Manual review remains for subtracting already-counted general-major credits from 심화전공 credits and for Co-op grandfathering by activity term.

Example: `ME.20000`, `ME.20005`, `ME.30040`, and `ME.40000` are the reviewed fixed required slots for the 2025 slice.

## CS

- `2019`: last in-scope CS admission slice before the capstone-team-project rule becomes mandatory.
- `2020`: first slice where the capstone pool becomes mandatory for 주전공 and 복수전공.
- `2021`: adds explicit `URP495` / 타 학과 개별연구 / `IS500` elective-recognition rules.
- `2022`: keeps the 2021 core and adds SoC Co-op handling for previously completed project/research courses.
- `2023-2024`: adds `CoE` elective recognition up to 6 credits, expands AI concentration options, and moves SoC Co-op into the 21-credit replacement family; 2024 also adds the optional information-security specialization note.
- `2025`: same rule family as 2023-2024 but with dotted codes (`CS.20004`, `CS.20006`, ...).
- Manual review remains for URP/IS500 provenance and SoC Co-op timing/history effects.

Example: `CS204` / `CS.20004` can be satisfied by `MAS275` / `MAS.20075` or `MA260` / `MA.20060`, and `CS311` / `CS.30101` can be satisfied by `EE312` / `EE.30012`.

## EE

- `2019-2021`: reviewed from the explicit `2018학년도 이후` EE section, not the broader `2016학년도 이후` section.
- `2022`: keeps the 2018+ EE core but adds retroactive 자유선택 treatment when EE Co-op follows already-completed design/research courses.
- `2023-2024`: introduces the `2023학년도 이후` EE section with 138 total credits while preserving the 15-credit required-major family and switching EE Co-op to the 21-credit family.
- `2025`: same rule family as 2023-2024 but with dotted codes (`EE.30005`, `EE.40005`, ...).
- Manual review remains for EE Co-op because the free-elective portion is program-evaluation dependent even after the 2023 rule refresh.

Example: the reviewed EE required-major structure is two fixed slots (`EE305`/`EE405` or dotted equivalents) plus any 3 of `EE201`, `EE202`, `EE204`, `EE209`, `EE210`, `EE211` in the matching code family slice.
